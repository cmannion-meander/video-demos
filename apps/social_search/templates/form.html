How a Tool Like Humantic AI Works

Let's break down how a tool like Humantic AI analyzes a resume into simple steps:

  1. Upload Your Resume: You start by uploading your resume into the tool.
  2. Extract Information: The tool reads your resume and picks out important details like your name, contact info, job titles, and skills.
  3. Run Searches: It uses this information to search for your online presence on sites like LinkedIn, Twitter, and Facebook.
  4. Analyze Content: The tool reads your social media posts and other online content to understand your personality using DISC and OCEAN frameworks.
  5. Generate Insights: It combines this information to give a detailed report about your personality and professional behavior.
  6. Create a Profile: Finally, the tool creates a profile that highlights your strengths, skills, and areas where you can improve.
