What is Happening Here?

In this step, we are taking the information from your resume and using it to search for your online presence. Here's a simple breakdown of the process:

  1. Resume Parsing: We take the resume you uploaded and extract important details like your name, job titles, and skills.
  2. Online Search: Using these details, we search for your profiles and activities on social media sites like LinkedIn, Twitter, and Facebook.
  3. Gather Information: We gather snippets of information from your social media profiles to understand more about your professional activities and personality.

This process extracts all publicly available information about you and helps to create a personality profile based on that online presence without taking a personality test. In the next section, we'll create that personality profile.
